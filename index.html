02

SCNPS Midlands Chapter

Inherited a disorganized, inconsistent, non-strategic marketing presence in April 2025. Currently rebuilding it from the ground up with a focus on growing, converting, and retaining membership. This is live portfolio work.

The problem

No system. No strategy. No baseline.

The chapter had a fragmented presence across channels with no consistent voice, no visual identity, no content calendar, and no way to measure what was working. Board roles had unclear interfaces with marketing.

The approach

Audit first. Build systems before content.

Conducted audience and psychographic research. Defined SMART membership goals. Mapped board roles to data, budget, and content functions. Built frameworks before producing a single asset.
